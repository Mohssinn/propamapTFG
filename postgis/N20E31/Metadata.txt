The European Settlement Map (ESM_BUILT_VHR2015_EUROPE_R2019_3035_02_V1_0) is the latest release of the European Settlement Map (ESM) produced in the frame of the GHSL project. 
The ESM product exploits the Copernicus VHR_IMAGE_2015 dataset made of satellite images Pleaiades, Deimos-02, WorldView-2, WorldView-3, GeoEye-01 and Spot 6/7 ranging from 2014 to 2016.
